about us

Life is hectic and we know that.
WallyCal allows you to seamlessly synchronize all your calendars into one simple and intuitive platform.
When life becomes busy, WallyCal is at your side.

SMART HOME TECHNOLOGY

WallyCal is a smart home device that synchronizes your calendar with Wi-Fi.

SIMPLICITY

WallyCal’s User Interface is simple and intuitive to use by everyday consumers.

CUSTOMIZABLE

WallyCal is fully customizable. Users can change the layout and background to their preference.

MODERN
DESIGN

WallyCal’s modern design will fit into any room in your home.

App

WallyCal provides an iOS/Android compatible app that will allow seamless integration from multiple calendar platforms from google calendar, outlook calendar, and many others. The App synchronizes to your WallyCal through your home Wifi, allowing you to get real time updates.
